# Enforcement & Integrity

This platform does not enforce behavior through spectacle.
It enforces integrity through structure, friction, and quiet consequence.

The goal of enforcement is not punishment.
The goal is preservation of reality.

---

## Core Enforcement Principle

**Violations are handled without amplification.**

No callouts.
No public shaming.
No algorithmic penalties disguised as “feedback.”

Integrity failures are resolved quietly, proportionally,
and with minimal disruption to surrounding journeys.

---

## What Is Enforced

### 1. Human-Origin Content

All entries must originate from a human lived experience.

The following are prohibited:
- AI-generated video or audio
- AI-generated narration, scripting, or reconstruction
- Synthetic personas or simulated lives

Detection may include:
- Pattern analysis across entries
- Temporal continuity checks
- Manual review when anomalies persist

Certainty is required before action.
Suspicion alone is insufficient.

---

### 2. Continuity & Authenticity

Journeys must demonstrate:
- Temporal coherence
- Consistent authorship
- Absence of sudden performative shifts tied to monetization

Indicators of violation:
- Abrupt tone or format changes aligned with commercial outcomes
- Insertion of promotional behavior without contextual grounding
- Manufactured arcs or exaggerated narrative pivots

The platform does not enforce *quality*.
It enforces *coherence*.

---

### 3. Covert Promotion & Manipulation

The following are disallowed:
- Undisclosed sponsorships
- Scripted product placement
- External incentives shaping content behavior
- Engagement farming disguised as documentation

Traceable commerce must emerge organically
from repeated presence within a journey.

Any attempt to bypass provenance rules is treated as a violation of trust.

---

## Enforcement Process

### Step 1 — Soft Friction

When anomalies are detected:
- Reduced discoverability (non-visible to the user)
- Removal from exploration surfaces
- No notification unless behavior persists

This stage is corrective, not punitive.

---

### Step 2 — Direct Review

If issues continue:
- Human review is initiated
- Context across the full journey is examined
- Creator may be contacted privately for clarification

No public indicators are applied.

---

### Step 3 — Containment

For confirmed violations:
- Monetization privileges may be suspended
- Provenance links may be removed
- Entries may be delisted from public exploration

Existing saved archives remain intact to preserve historical context.

---

### Step 4 — Removal (Last Resort)

Only in cases of repeated or severe violations:
- Journey is frozen
- No new entries allowed
- Existing content may remain accessible without amplification

Deletion is avoided unless legally required.

---

## What Is *Not* Enforced

The platform does not enforce:
- Frequency of posting
- Aesthetic standards
- Narrative clarity
- Likeability
- Growth rate
- Emotional tone

Boredom is allowed.
Messiness is allowed.
Silence is allowed.

---

## Appeals & Corrections

Creators may request review of enforcement actions.

Appeals are:
- Private
- Contextual
- Handled by humans

Corrections are preferred over removals whenever possible.

---

## Platform Responsibility

The platform acknowledges its own power.

Therefore:
- Enforcement systems must be auditable internally
- Automation must never be the final authority
- Revenue considerations must never override integrity decisions

If enforcing truth becomes inconvenient,
truth still takes precedence.

---

## Closing Clause

This platform survives only if trust compounds over time.

Enforcement exists not to control users,
but to ensure that when someone follows a journey,
what they are seeing is still real.
