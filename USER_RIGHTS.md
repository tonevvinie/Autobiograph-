# User Rights Charter

This platform exists to preserve lived reality.
Users are not customers of attention systems.
They are custodians of their own records.

The following rights are fundamental and non-negotiable.

---

## 1. Right to Context Integrity

Users have the right for their Entries to remain
unaltered, unoptimized, and contextually intact.

The platform may not:
- Edit Entries
- Reorder Entries to change meaning
- Add narration, summaries, or reinterpretation
- Alter presentation for engagement or monetization

What is recorded is what stands.

---

## 2. Right to Authorship

Users retain authorship of all Entries they create.

This includes:
- Creative authorship
- Contextual authorship
- Temporal authorship

The platform does not claim ownership of lived experience.
It provides infrastructure for preservation only.

---

## 3. Right to Non-Performance

Users have the right to exist on the platform
without performing for visibility.

This includes the right to:
- Post irregularly or infrequently
- Be boring
- Be quiet
- Be unfinished
- Pause without penalty

No user may be disadvantaged for refusing optimization.

---

## 4. Right to Silence

Users have the right to stop.

Stopping may include:
- Pausing a journey
- Ending a journey
- Archiving a journey
- Leaving Entries untouched indefinitely

Silence is treated as a valid state, not decay.

---

## 5. Right to Traceability Without Exploitation

Users may allow provenance links to emerge from their journeys.

They also have the right to:
- Decline provenance links
- Remove provenance links
- Prevent commercial traceability entirely

Traceability must never be forced or incentivized.

---

## 6. Right to Monetization Without Distortion

If users choose to monetize:
- Monetization must follow existing behavior
- No feature may pressure increased output
- No feature may reward exaggeration or narrative shaping

Users may earn without becoming performers.

---

## 7. Right to Privacy and Containment

Users have the right to:
- Keep Boards private
- Limit discoverability
- Remove Entries from exploration surfaces
- Archive content without public exposure

Visibility is optional.
Context is mandatory.

---

## 8. Right to Explanation

If enforcement actions affect a user:
- They are entitled to a private explanation
- They may request human review
- They may correct behavior without public consequence

Automation may assist, but humans remain accountable.

---

## 9. Right to Exit With Dignity

Users may leave the platform.

Upon exit:
- Entries may be exported in original form
- Archives remain intact unless the user requests removal
- No shadow retention for behavioral analysis

Leaving is not treated as betrayal.

---

## 10. Right to a Stable Social Contract

Users have the right to expect that:
- Core definitions will not be reinterpreted silently
- Monetization rules will not drift covertly
- Enforcement standards will not change without record

If rules change, they must be documented.

---

## Closing Statement

This platform does not promise growth, fame, or reach.

It promises:
- Preservation over performance
- Continuity over virality
- Context over optimization

These rights exist to ensure that promise survives
pressure, scale, and time.
