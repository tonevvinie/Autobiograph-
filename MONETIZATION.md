# Monetization Philosophy

This platform does not monetize attention.
It monetizes continuity, traceability, and preserved reality.

All monetization mechanisms must reinforce truth, originality, and lived experience.
Any mechanism that incentivizes performance, distortion, or artificial behavior is forbidden.

---

## Core Principle

**Money may follow reality, but must never shape it.**

Creators are not performers.
Viewers are not targets.
Journeys are not products.

---

## Allowed Monetization Models

### 1. Journey Patronage

Users may support ongoing journeys, not creators as personalities.

Patronage grants:
- Access to extended continuity (earlier entries, longer timelines)
- Ability to save or archive entire journeys for personal reference
- Quiet acknowledgment within the journey context (no badges, no public counts)

Patronage must never:
- Unlock exclusive content that changes creator behavior
- Incentivize increased posting frequency
- Create social hierarchy or status signaling

---

### 2. Traceable Commerce (Provenance Links)

Objects, tools, places, or processes that appear naturally within entries may be linked through **provenance markers**.

Rules:
- Links are contextual, not promotional
- They appear only when an object is repeatedly present in a journey
- Viewers must opt in to follow object trails

The platform earns revenue for maintaining traceability, not persuasion.

No ads.
No recommendations.
No sponsored placement.

---

### 3. Archival Licensing

Completed or long-running journeys may be archived as coherent records.

Archives may be licensed for:
- Education
- Research
- Documentary reference
- Historical preservation

Conditions:
- Creator consent is mandatory
- Archives are frozen (no edits, no retroactive polishing)
- Context must remain intact

Truth must age without manipulation.

---

### 4. Verification as a Service

The platform may provide verification of:
- Human-origin content
- Continuity over time
- Absence of AI-generated material
- Unbroken authorship

Verification services may be licensed to:
- Educational institutions
- Publishers
- Documentarians
- Research organizations

Verification certifies reality, not quality or popularity.

---

## Forbidden Monetization

The following are permanently disallowed:

- Advertising based on engagement or attention
- Influencer sponsorships
- Paid boosts, trending placement, or visibility manipulation
- Creator performance incentives
- Gamified rewards tied to frequency, virality, or growth
- Selling user data or behavioral profiles

---

## Economic Intent

This platform is designed to grow slowly.

Value increases with:
- Time
- Continuity
- Specificity
- Trust

Revenue is a byproduct of preserved reality, not engineered behavior.

---

## Non-Negotiable Clause

If a monetization mechanism pressures users to:
- Perform
- Exaggerate
- Optimize themselves
- Misrepresent lived experience

It must be rejected, regardless of revenue potential.

---

**Reality comes first.  
Money is allowed only if it follows quietly behind.**
