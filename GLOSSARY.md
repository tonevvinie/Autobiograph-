# Glossary of Core Concepts

This document defines the foundational terms used across the platform.
These definitions are normative, not descriptive.

If implementation or interpretation conflicts with these definitions,
the implementation is wrong.

---

## Entry

An **Entry** is the smallest atomic unit of lived reality on the platform.

An Entry is:
- A single recorded moment
- Captured in real time
- Authored by one human
- Minimally edited (trim only)

An Entry represents **evidence**, not performance.

An Entry is not:
- A highlight
- A recap
- A scripted segment
- A message optimized for an audience

All higher-level structures (boards, journeys, archives, monetization)
are composed exclusively of Entries.
No structure may alter or reinterpret an Entry after creation.

---

## Board / Journey

A **Board** (also referred to as a Journey) is a contextual container
that groups Entries by lived continuity.

A Board:
- Preserves temporal order
- Represents an ongoing or bounded phase of life
- May be public or private
- May evolve slowly over time

Boards do not imply narrative intent.
Meaning emerges from accumulation, not design.

---

## Provenance Link

A **Provenance Link** is a traceable reference connecting lived experience
to an external object, place, tool, or process.

A Provenance Link:
- Appears only through repeated contextual presence
- Is non-promotional and non-recommendational
- Requires opt-in interaction from the viewer
- Exists to preserve traceability, not influence behavior

Provenance Links are never:
- Advertisements
- Sponsored placements
- Optimized for conversion
- Inserted manually for monetization purposes

They exist solely to answer the question:
“Where did this come from, and how do we know?”

---

## Archive

An **Archive** is a completed or time-bounded Board
that has been intentionally preserved in a frozen state.

An Archive:
- Cannot be edited, reordered, or optimized
- Preserves original temporal sequence
- Retains full contextual integrity
- Represents a finished or historically valuable record

Archives are not content libraries.
They are records.

Archives may be licensed or referenced
only with creator consent and without modification.

---

## Save

A **Save** is the primary social interaction on the platform.

Saving:
- Indicates personal relevance or continuity interest
- Does not signal popularity, approval, or status
- Is silent and non-performative

Save counts are contextual, not competitive.
They exist to support discovery through resonance,
not to rank or reward creators.

---

## Continuity

**Continuity** refers to coherence over time.

Continuity is demonstrated through:
- Repetition without performance
- Consistent authorship
- Temporal integrity
- Absence of sudden optimization shifts

Continuity is enforced structurally, not aesthetically.

---

## Authenticity

On this platform, **Authenticity** is not an expression of sincerity.
It is a property of structure.

Authenticity emerges when:
- Entries remain unaltered
- Journeys unfold without optimization pressure
- Monetization follows rather than shapes behavior

Authenticity cannot be claimed.
It can only be preserved.

---

## Closing Note

These terms define the operating reality of the platform.

They are designed to:
- Resist trend drift
- Prevent semantic dilution
- Preserve trust over time

If new features require redefining these terms,
the feature must be rejected.
