# Third-Party Commerce & Protective Redirection

This platform does not host transactions.
It protects traceability by redirecting users to trusted external marketplaces.

Commerce is permitted only through provenance,
and provenance must terminate in verified environments.

---

## Core Principle

**The platform preserves reality.  
Transactions occur elsewhere, safely.**

By refusing to intermediate sales directly,
the platform avoids conflicts of interest,
prevents pressure on creators,
and protects users from fraud and coercion.

---

## Provenance-Based Redirection

When a Provenance Link connects to an object, tool, place, or product:

- The link redirects users to an external, vetted marketplace
- The platform never processes payments
- The platform never stores financial credentials
- The platform never hosts storefronts or checkouts

The role of the platform ends at **traceability**, not conversion.

---

## Vetted Marketplaces Only

Provenance Links may redirect only to marketplaces that meet
strict verification and trust standards.

Minimum requirements include:
- Established buyer protection policies
- Clear refund and dispute resolution mechanisms
- Transparent seller identity and accountability
- Compliance with regional consumer protection laws

Examples include:
- Established e-commerce platforms
- Direct-to-consumer storefronts with verified fulfillment
- Physical locations with verifiable presence

Unverified, opaque, or exploitative marketplaces are prohibited.

---

## Creator Protection

Creators may sell products or services only through
approved third-party platforms.

This ensures:
- Legal and financial protection for creators
- Separation between documentation and selling
- Reduced pressure to perform or upsell
- Clear boundaries between life and commerce

The platform does not allow:
- In-app selling
- Direct payment requests
- Informal transaction routing
- Undisclosed affiliate manipulation

Creators document.
Marketplaces transact.

---

## User Protection

Users are protected by design.

This includes:
- Clear indication that they are leaving the platform
- Visibility into the destination marketplace
- Assurance that transactions occur under external buyer protections

The platform never implies endorsement.
It provides context, not guarantees.

---

## Platform Responsibility

The platform is responsible for:
- Vetting and maintaining a list of trusted marketplaces
- Removing provenance links to destinations that lose trustworthiness
- Preventing circumvention through disguised links or redirects

Revenue earned through provenance must never
compromise vetting standards.

---

## Forbidden Practices

The following are explicitly disallowed:
- Direct sales hosted on the platform
- Unvetted or anonymous marketplaces
- Pay-to-place provenance links
- Dynamic link swapping for optimization
- Conversion tracking that profiles users

If a monetization mechanism requires surveillance,
it is incompatible with the platform.

---

## Closing Clause

This platform protects users by knowing where its responsibility ends.

By enforcing redirection to trusted marketplaces,
it ensures that:
- Reality remains uncontaminated
- Commerce remains accountable
- Trust does not become a liability
