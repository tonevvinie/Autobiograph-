# Vision

Record exists to preserve truth and originality in human self-documentation.

This platform is not for performance, persuasion, or optimization.
It is for lived moments, recorded honestly, over time.

We believe:
- truth survives time better than spectacle
- originality comes from constraint, not novelty
- silence is more meaningful than reaction
- boredom is a feature, not a failure

If growth, monetization, or popularity ever conflict with truth,
truth wins.
